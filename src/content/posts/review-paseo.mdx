## The pitch

Paseo is a self-hosted daemon that runs your coding agents and exposes them
through one interface, desktop, mobile, web, or CLI. Run several in parallel,
hand work between them, and drive it all from wherever you are.

## Where it shines

If you already live across Claude Code, Codex, and Copilot, Paseo collapses them
into one control surface and lets you start a task at your desk and check it from
your phone. The orchestration skills, handoff, loop, committee, are the
genuinely novel part.

## Where it bites

It is early and maintained by one person. The ideas are ahead of the polish, and
AGPL-3.0 plus self-hosting means it is not a drop-in for every team. Watch it; do
not yet bet a critical workflow on it.
