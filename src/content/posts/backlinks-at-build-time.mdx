Backlinks are generated at build time from four sources: manual frontmatter
relations, internal links, supersedes/supersededBy fields, and shared topics.

```ts
const edges = posts.flatMap((p) => [
  ...p.related.map((r) => link(p, r)),
  ...internalLinks(p),
]);
```

- Manual related links first
- Direct internal-link backlinks second
- Then "more in this topic"

<Callout type="tip" title="Keep it useful, not exhaustive">
A backlink section that lists everything is noise. Cap it, rank it, and let the
graph page handle full discovery.
</Callout>
